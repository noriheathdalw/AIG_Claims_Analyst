# AIG_Claims_Analyst

AIG Underwriting Virtual Job Simulation through Forage! This hands-on experience gave me a deeper understanding of what a career in insurance underwriting looks like- combining analytical thinking, commercial awareness and relationship-building skills. 

🧠 Key take-aways from the programme: 

Client presentation task:
  
  - Researched Lexington Healthcare's insurance offerings
  - Built a one-page client presentation showcasing the team's strengths
  - Gained insight into how underwriters communicate value and manage client expectations.

Underwriting task: 

  - Reviewed a healthcare facility's application
  - Assessed exposures, claims history and financials
  - Made pricing and coverage recommendations based on risk analysis

💡 What stood out to me most was how underwriting brings together both data-driven decision-making and human judgment. It is a dynamic role that plays a crucial part in protecting businesses while supporting growth. 

This experience has only deepened my interest in the insurance industry and reaffirmed my enthusiasm to explore it further. Grateful to platforms like Forage for making opportunities like this accessible to students and early-career professionals.
